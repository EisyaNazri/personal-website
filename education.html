My Education Journey

I started school when I was 5 years old, many bitter and sweet memories that have matured me. These are the schools that I have attended from the beginning until now to study:

Sekolah Kebangsaan Laloh (SK Laloh)

Pra Sekolah Permata SK Laloh

This is where I started my schooling at Pra Permata SK Laloh.

I started school here in 2009-2010 and at that time I was 5-6 years old.

Every day, I will go to school early in the morning.

Although it has been many years since this event, there are many sweet memories that I have built there.

 2 years

Sekolah Kebangsaan Laloh (SK Laloh)

Here, that is at SK Laloh, I started learning to be independent.

I studied there from 2011-2016, from Grade 1 to 6.

Every day, I went to school riding a bicycle, and I sat for several important exams at that time, namely the Kafa class assessment test in 2015 and the primary school assessment test in year 2016 or better known as UPKK and UPSR.

Here too, many sweet and bitter memories are still fresh in my mind.

 6 years

Sekolah Menengah Ugama (Arab) Darus Salam (SMAD)

I moved to secondary school which is at SMU (A) Darus Salam (SMAD) which is located quite far from my home.

Therefore, I have to stay in the hostel due to transportation problems.

I have sat for several important exams at that school in 2019-2021, namely Penilaian Tingkatan 3 (PT3), Sijil Menengah Ugama (SMU) and Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM).

I studied here from 2017 to 2021.

 5 years

Maahad Rahmaniah Kuala Krai (MRKK)

Next I moved to school in Maahad Rahmaniah Kuala Krai (MRKK) to take the Sijil Tinggi Agama Malaysia or better known as STAM.

Not many take this exam because many subjects must be memorized in Arabic and must pass all subjects to get the certificate.

The students in my class at that time were only 11 of the 30 people at the beginning.

Although the time was relatively short, which was only a year, but many beautiful and bitter memories were engraved there.

 1 year

UiTM Kedah

After that, I continued my education journey by taking a degree.

Therefore, I filled out the UPU online and selected several different costs.

Grateful to Allah, I got the opportunity to continue my studies at UiTM Kedah with the cost that I have chosen in the UPU application which is Bachelor of Information Science (Honours) Library Management (CDIM260).

It is my 6th choice in the UPU selection list.

 2023 until now
